Got the date, but not the ideas?

This website is designed to help people who already bagged a date, but have no clue what to cook up for their significant other. Press the button and you will get recipe for a main meal, drink and a playlist (to set the mood).

But beware! The mix may be more than you have bargained for as it can be very unpredictable, but persevere and you might just end up with a best date you've ever had (or at least the funniest one!).

This is a final project for Code First: Girls by Lana, Lydia and Kristina
